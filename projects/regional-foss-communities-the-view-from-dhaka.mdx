# Learning From Open Source Communities to Combat Harassment

Studies of code repositories often suggest little open source
activity originating in South Asia, yet regional communities
continue to form around major projects.

What is the nature of participation in open source software in
these regional communities? What drives their formation? How
might we leverage and better support them to strengthen the
open source ecosystem?
